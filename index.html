The Rhythm Of Data

Data Investigated by Yihang, Yiheng, Yiming & Yuyang

1. The song duration distribution is right-skewed, centered around 4-5 minutes, with moderate spread and several high-end outliers.

2. The average song duration is 3.97 minutes. Song lengths typically deviate from this mean by about 1.26 minutes.

3. The Empirical Rule fails: observed proportions within 1, 2, and 3 SDs miss 68-95-99.7%, confirming the right-skewed shape.
